AI Executive Digest V1 PRD

1) Product Goal
Build a daily, automated AI market/news briefing for executives, focused on business impact over technical depth.

2) Audience
Mixed audience of tech and business executives.

3) V1 Scope
- One daily segment: Fintech/Banking.
- Public subscribe/unsubscribe flow.
- Admin area to add/remove subscribers and sources.
- Automated ingestion from RSS + one discovery API.
- Automated LLM synthesis of top 3 business-impact stories.
- Scheduled generation, then scheduled send.
- Optional HITL: admin may review and skip/approve before send.

4) Non-Goals
- Multi-industry personalization in V1.
- Complex user roles/permissions.
- Multi-database architecture.

5) Functional Requirements
- Collect subscriber emails from web UI.
- Maintain subscriber list in one database.
- Ingest and deduplicate source content.
- Generate structured digest with source links.
- Send digest to active subscribers using Resend.
- Keep run/send logs.
- Admin toggle for HITL requirement.

6) Reliability Requirements
- Idempotent cron endpoints.
- Safe re-run without duplicate sends.
- Graceful behavior if source/API errors occur.

7) Success Criteria
- Daily digest is generated and delivered on schedule.
- Digest includes 3 high-signal fintech/banking items.
- Admin can enable review mode and block send until approval.
